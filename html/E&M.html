Maxwells equations

Biot Savart law

  • $B = \frac{\mu_0NI}{r}$

Poynting vector

  • $\vec{S} = \frac{1}{\mu_0} \vec{E}\times\vec{B}$

Faraday's law of induction

  • A change in magnetic field gives rise to an induced current
    • $$\varepsilon = \frac{-Nd\Phi_B}{dt}$$
  • As area $A$ increases, flux $\Phi$ increases
  • The flux is $\Phi = B\cdot A = BA\mathrm{cos}\theta$
  • the current from the induced emf is $I = \frac{\varepsilon}{R}$ and the power from the induced current can be given by $P = I^2R$
